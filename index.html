Features

Personal Branding

With all our features, we give you the tools to help you develop your personal brand. We've studied the best creators and built their best practices into our tools. Our app offers you a structure that will help you set weekly objectives to achieve consistency and growth. This is how we make sure that everything we do is focused on your success.

Content Engine

Our platform is designed to be a content engine where everything is done at the same place. You can activate blitz mode to generate ideas if you're in a creative rut, draft and improve your posts before scheduling them, or even use templates to create engaging content every time.

We created it to make the process as smooth as possible so you don't spend hours on it

AI

Finding the right words to help you grow your personal brand can be tough. When it comes to your content, it's important to have an original voice while making sure that your audience gets the content they need to hear. We've designed our tool to use AI to support your creative processes by giving you inspiration, helping you write faster, and much more.

Scheduling and Analytics

Scheduling and analytics are crucial to the success of any content strategy. With Brandist, you can schedule your content for LinkedIn and Twitter, and see how your posts have performed. This way you can make sure you're always putting out the kind of content that gets you closer to your goals!

The way you pay is in feedback — the product will be free forever for the first 100 subscribers.

About Us

We wanted to create a platform where anyone can build an awesome personal brand, regardless of their previous experience.

We were two ex-colleagues who found ourselves in the same position: one looking to share his knowledge and one looking to build his notoriety so he could get more leads. But we had the same problem: we felt like we didn’t have the skills to create a great brand for ourselves, and it made us feel overwhelmed and stuck.

So, we created Brandist so you don’t have that feeling! By mixing best practices with technology, we believe that anyone has the power to create their own reputation and voice in the world.
