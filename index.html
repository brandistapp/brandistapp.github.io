About Us

We want to create a platform where anyone can build an awesome personal brand, regardless of their previous experience.

We are two ex-colleagues who found ourselves in the same position: one looking to share his knowledge and one looking to build his notoriety so he could get more leads. But we have the same problem: we feel like we don’t have the skills to create a great brand for ourselves, and it makes us feel overwhelmed and stuck.

So, we are creating Brandist so you don’t have that feeling! By mixing best practices with technology, we believe that anyone has the power to create their own reputation and voice in the world.
